Privacy Policy for Fuel Near Me

Last Updated: June 2026

Fuel Near Me ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Fuel Near Me mobile application.

1. Location Information

Fuel Near Me uses your device's location to help you find nearby:

  • Petrol Stations
  • CNG/LPG Stations
  • EV Charging Stations

Location data is used only to provide search results and is not permanently stored or shared by us.

2. Local Storage

The application stores certain preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Theme preference (Light/Dark mode)
  • Language settings
  • Default search radius
  • Favorite stations

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Voice Search and Microphone Access

Fuel Near Me may request microphone permission when you use the voice search feature. Audio is used only to convert speech into text and is not recorded or stored by us. Microphone access is used solely to improve the search experience.

4. Advertisements

Fuel Near Me uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics

Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following services:

  • Google Maps Platform
  • Google Places API
  • Google Geocoding API
  • Google AdMob

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Fuel Near Me is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
